Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

sumofme is a professional profile app that lets you create and share a public page with your name, contact details, and résumé. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is handled.

Information You Provide

When you set up your profile, you may provide:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Résumé or work history

You control exactly what appears on your profile. You are never required to provide all of this information.

Your Profile is Public

Information you add to your sumofme profile is publicly accessible to anyone with your profile link. Only publish information you are comfortable sharing publicly.

Your public profile is served through a Next.js web application. Anyone who has your profile URL can view the information you have chosen to display — no login is required to view it.

Data Storage

Your profile data is stored in your iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework. I do not have access to your data. CloudKit keeps your information tied to your Apple ID and managed under Apple's privacy practices.

No Tracking or Analytics

I do not collect usage analytics, track your behavior, or share your data with advertisers or any third parties beyond what is needed to display your public profile.

Your Control

You can update or remove any information from your profile at any time from within the app. Removing your profile immediately takes it offline. You can also delete the app and manage iCloud storage via Settings › Apple ID › iCloud.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
