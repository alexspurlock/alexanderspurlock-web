Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This policy describes how Dishboard handles your data. The short version: your data stays on your device and in your iCloud account — I can't see any of it.

Your Data Stays With You

Dishboard stores all data — including your meal plans, recipes, and preferences — in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. I have no ability to access, view, or retrieve your data. All storage and sync is managed entirely by Apple according to their own privacy practices.

No Data Collection

I do not collect, store, or process any personal information. Dishboard has no accounts, no logins, and no data is ever transmitted to my servers.

Third-Party Services

Dishboard does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Control

You can delete all app data at any time by deleting Dishboard or managing your iCloud storage in Settings › Apple ID › iCloud.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
