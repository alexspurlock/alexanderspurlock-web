Pocket Lead

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Pocket Lead is a lead management and AI-powered messaging tool for contractors. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and which third-party services may receive it.

Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, company name, and phone number.

Usage data

We track your number of AI-generated messages and your current subscription status to manage plan limits and billing.

Device information

We collect your device's push notification token to deliver reminders and in-app notifications.

Lead and customer data

You may enter information about your customers and leads, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, and notes. This data is provided by you and stored on your behalf. See "Your Responsibilities" below.

Third-Party Services

The following services receive data as part of normal app operation.

Firebase & Google

We use Firebase for user authentication and database storage. Your account information and lead data are stored on Firebase servers operated by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Gemini AI

When you use the AI message generation feature, data is sent to Google's servers. This includes your name and company information, the lead's name, the service or job type, and any notes you have entered for that lead. Do not enter sensitive personal information in notes you intend to use for AI generation.

This data is transmitted to Google's Gemini API to generate a suggested message. Google processes this data under their API terms of service.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives your subscription status and transaction history. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple

Subscription payments are processed by Apple. Apple handles payment information under their own privacy policy. We never see or store your payment details.

Local Notifications

Appointment and follow-up reminders are scheduled entirely on your device using iOS's local notification system. No data is sent to any server for this feature.

Your Responsibilities

Pocket Lead allows you to store information about your customers and leads — people who have not directly agreed to your use of this app.

You are solely responsible for:

  • Having a lawful basis (such as consent or legitimate business interest) to store your customers' data in this app
  • Complying with applicable privacy laws (such as CCPA, GDPR, or other local regulations) regarding the data you enter
  • Ensuring your customers are appropriately informed about how their information is used

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We will post any updates to this policy at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of Pocket Lead after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
