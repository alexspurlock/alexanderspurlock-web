Pocket Lead

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Who We Are

Pocket Lead is a US-based app for contractors and service professionals to manage leads and generate follow-up messages. Questions about this policy can be sent to alexanderspurlock@gmail.com.

Age Requirement

Pocket Lead is intended for users 16 years of age or older. By using the app, you confirm that you meet this requirement.

What We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, company name, and phone number.

Lead and customer data

Information you enter about your customers and leads, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, budgets, notes, and activity logs.

Usage data

We track your number of AI-generated messages and your current subscription status to manage plan limits and features.

Device information

We collect your device's push notification token to deliver reminders and in-app notifications.

How We Use Your Data

To provide the app and its features

Your account and lead data are used to power the core functionality of Pocket Lead, including storing your leads, tracking activity, and syncing across your devices.

AI message generation

When you use the AI message feature, lead context — including the lead's name, the service or job type, notes, and activity — is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a suggested follow-up message. This data leaves your device and is processed on Google's servers.

Subscription management

Usage data and subscription status are used to enforce plan limits and manage your subscription through RevenueCat.

Third-Party Services

Google Firebase

We use Firebase for user authentication and database storage. Your account and lead data are stored on Firebase servers operated by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Gemini AI

Lead context is sent to Gemini's API to generate follow-up messages. Google processes this data under their API terms of service.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage and track subscription status. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store & Google Play

Payments are processed by Apple and Google through their respective app stores. We never receive or store your payment card details.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in the United States.

Data Retention

Your data is retained after your subscription ends and remains accessible if you resubscribe. Your data is permanently deleted when you delete your account. An account deletion option is available within the app.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to know — you may request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected about you.
  • Right to deletion — you may delete your data at any time by deleting your account within the app.
  • No sale of data — we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

To exercise these rights, contact us at alexanderspurlock@gmail.com.

Push Notifications

You can opt out of push notifications at any time through your device settings. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to use the app.

Changes to This Policy

When this policy is updated, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Pocket Lead after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, reach us at alexanderspurlock@gmail.com.
